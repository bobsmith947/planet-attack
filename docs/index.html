Planet Attack is a Tetris-like puzzle where the blocks fall towards the center of the screen, and you need to from a complete ring in order to clear blocks.

All of the classic Tetris pieces are available to build with, and the next 3 upcoming pieces are shown to help you better strategize your placement.

You can also choose to save a piece for later placement with the hold feature.

The controls are very easy to use:

  • Pieces can be moved left-right or up-down (depending on the direction it falls in) by dragging.
  • Pieces can be rotated by tapping.
    • Pieces will rotate to the right if possible.
    • If rotating a piece will cause it to overlap with an existing piece, it will attempt to move to an adjacent empty spot.
    • Otherwise the rotation will fail.
  • Pieces can be automatically dropped by pressing and holding.
